About Aris Dascalu




Aris Dascalu is the visionary Founder and CEO of Berries Technologies, a company at the forefront of the interconnected fields of cybersecurity, AI, and software development.
With a passion for leveraging technology to solve complex problems, Aris has built Berries Technologies from the ground up, establishing it as a key player known for its commitment to excellence and a forward-thinking approach. Under his leadership, Berries Technologies has achieved significant milestones, including growing its user base to over 5 million users across the globe.
Aris's strategic acumen and deep understanding of market dynamics have been instrumental in driving the company's growth and shaping its reputation for delivering impactful, reliable, and user-centric solutions.

Aris is a strong advocate for responsible and ethical practices and fostering a culture of community building.
His leadership philosophy centers on empowering his team to push creative boundaries and foster a culture of continuous learning and innovation. He is dedicated to building a company that not only leads its industry but also makes a positive contribution to society.

Prior to founding Berries Technologies, Aris started his career as a Network Operations Center (NOC) engineer.
His hands-on experience and diverse background in the technology sector provide the foundation for his strategic vision and his ability to navigate the ever-evolving technological landscape.
